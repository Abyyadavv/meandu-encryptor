How it works

  1. Type your message and enter a password, then click Encrypt.
  2. Copy the encrypted text from the result box using Copy Encrypted.
  3. After copying, the fields are reset for security.
  4. To decrypt, paste the encrypted text, enter the password, then click Decrypt.
